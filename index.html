Welcome to Krishna Tissues Pvt. Ltd.

Krishna Tissues Pvt. Ltd. is one of India's leading manufacturers of premium quality paperboard products. Since its incorporation in 2005, the company has continuously grown by combining innovation, quality, and sustainable manufacturing practices.

Our products are manufactured using 100% recycled paper, making us an environmentally responsible organization committed to delivering eco-friendly packaging solutions.

Who We Are

From a small paper trading business to becoming one of India's fastest growing paperboard manufacturers, Krishna Tissues has built its reputation through commitment, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Today our products are used in everyday life including medicine boxes, toothpaste packaging, garment boxes, cosmetic packaging, retail cartons, food packaging, publishing, stationery, and industrial packaging.

Our Vision

To provide 100% eco-friendly solutions for the packaging industry while maintaining international quality standards and sustainable manufacturing practices.

Our Guiding Philosophy

Krishna Tissues is a customer-centric organization focused on continuous product development, quality improvement, innovation, and cost-effective manufacturing.

Every decision we make is centered around providing customers with high-quality products at competitive prices.
