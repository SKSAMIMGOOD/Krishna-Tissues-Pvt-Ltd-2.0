About Us

Krishna Tissues Pvt. Ltd. is one of India's leading manufacturers of premium paperboard products and sustainable packaging solutions.

Since its establishment in 2005, the company has consistently focused on innovation, quality, customer satisfaction, and environmentally responsible manufacturing.

Using modern technology and 100% recycled paper as raw material, Krishna Tissues manufactures world-class paperboards for domestic and international markets.

Our Story

Krishna Tissues Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated in March 2005.

Beginning as a small paper trading business, the company gradually expanded into a full-scale paperboard manufacturing organization.

Through continuous investment in technology, skilled manpower, and quality manufacturing, Krishna Tissues has become one of the fastest-growing companies in the Indian Paper Board Industry.

Today the company serves customers across India while exporting products to international markets, particularly in South-East Asia.

The company operates advanced manufacturing facilities capable of producing high-quality coated duplex boards and kraft liner boards for multiple industries.
